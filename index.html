🏭 About Us

Beginner's Field Nursey & Primary School (BFNPS)

A nurturing home for every child's first steps in learning

🎯

Our Mission

To provide every child with a strong academic foundation rooted in discipline, creativity and godly values — preparing them for a lifetime of learning and excellence.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the foremost early education institution in Lagos, producing well-rounded, confident and morally upright children who are ready to lead tomorrow's Nigeria.

📚 What We Offer

A Complete Early Education

From Nursery through Primary, we cover every aspect of your child's development

🧒

Nursery School

Nursery 1, 2 & 3 — structured play-based learning that builds language, numeracy and social skills in a warm, child-friendly setting.

📚

Primary School

Primary 1 to 6 — a rigorous and balanced academic programme covering Mathematics, English, Science, Social Studies, Yoruba and more.

⭐

Moral & Spiritual Values

We instil discipline, respect, honesty and godly character through weekly assemblies, guided conduct and a values-driven school culture.

🎨

Arts & Creativity

Drawing, crafts, music and drama are woven into our programme to nurture creativity, expression and confidence in every child.

🏃

Sports & Physical Education

Regular PE sessions, inter-house sports and outdoor activities keep children active, healthy and team-spirited all year round.

👪

Parent Partnership

Education is a team effort. PTA meetings, progress reports and our online portal keep parents informed and involved at every step.
